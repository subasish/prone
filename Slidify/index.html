Drivers can be divided into two broad categories: at-fault and not-at-fault drivers.

Crash prone drivers are those who are involved more than one crashes in a definite timeframe. In the app Vehicle 1 indicates the At-fault Crash Prone Drivers and Vehicle 2 indicates Not-At-Fault Crash Prone Drivers.
